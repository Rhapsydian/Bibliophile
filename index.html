What is Bibliophile?

Do you read whenever you have a spare moment? Do you have too many books and not enough time to read them all or struggle to keep track of what you’ve read and what your friends suggest you read? Bibliophile is designed for those that answered yes. With Bibliophile you can search for books and save them to your reading list. When you finish a book you can move it to the list of books you’ve already read so you’ll never forget the title of that fantastic book your want to recommend to your friends and family.
